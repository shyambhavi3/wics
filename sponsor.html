Support Us!

Over the last two years, a team of dedicated officers has transformed a dormant organization into a blossoming community of women in technology who help each other thrive in all aspects of student life, focusing on academic and professional excellence. ​


As a sponsor, you will have the opportunity to network with, and share the culture of your organization with students and allies of underrepresented genders studying Computer Science and Information Science, Technology, and Arts at the University of Arizona. By contributing to the growth of WICS, you will be bringing our mission to support underrepresented genders in technology to life. We accomplish this by creating a sense of community, helping each other explore topics in computing, and developing professional skills together. ​


We invite you to join our movement and are so thankful that you want to help our community and share your resources. ​
